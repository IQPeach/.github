## What IQPeach Does

IQPeach is a learning technology studio. We help schools, nonprofits, and education-focused teams design and ship digital systems that are easier to run and better for learners.

From strategy to launch, we build technology that teams can actually use every day.

## Core Capabilities

<details open>
  <summary><b>Custom Learning Platforms</b></summary>
  Purpose-built learning products designed around your users, curriculum, and workflows.
</details>

<details open>
  <summary><b>LMS Integrations and System Automation</b></summary>
  Cleaner operations, better data flow, and fewer manual tasks across your existing stack.
</details>

<details open>
  <summary><b>Analytics and Decision Dashboards</b></summary>
  Practical reporting systems that help educators and leaders make faster, informed decisions.
</details>

<details open>
  <summary><b>Learning Experience and Curriculum Technology</b></summary>
  Digital learning environments that improve engagement, retention, and outcomes.
</details>

## Delivery Model

| Stage | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| `1. Discovery` | Define goals, users, constraints, and measurable outcomes. |
| `2. Architecture` | Design product, data, and integration patterns. |
| `3. Build` | Ship in milestones with rapid feedback cycles. |
| `4. Launch and Support` | Stabilize, improve, and scale with your team. |
