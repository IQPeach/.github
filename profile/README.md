## Building learning technology that works in the real world

IQPeach designs and develops custom learning systems for schools, nonprofits, and mission-driven organizations.  
We combine product strategy, engineering, and instructional insight to deliver tools that improve outcomes and reduce operational friction.

## Our approach

| Phase | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| `1. Discovery` | Clarify goals, user constraints, and success metrics. |
| `2. Architecture` | Define system design, integrations, and implementation scope. |
